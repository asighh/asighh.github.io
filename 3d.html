details:

Type: Illustration

Completed: April 10, 2022

Tools: Sketchbook, Lead Pencil, Coloring Markers, Pinterest

Service: Illustrator

goal:

I wanted to practice drawing real people.

After spending a lot of time playing with procreate, I wanted to tap back into sketching and working with a simpler more straightforward medium.

challenges:

There weren't really any challenges as I just found reference photos on Pinterest to use.

I did not trace, I would look back and forth and try to copy the image by eye. I added blue and red lines on the face because the line, “I see red, I see blue, I get sad, angry too” was still influencing me. I built on this concept by adding purple and making black tears. Purple is a mix of red and blue, in this case being a mixture of anger and sadness. Tears are a result of extreme emotion. Tears are normally transparent but I made them black to represent a blend of negative emotions.

Additional Thoughts:

I added in this second drawing because initially, I was going to also add red and blue lines, but I decided against it in the end.

results:

These drawings were good practice and their simplicity and straightforwardness were very relaxing.
