details:

Type: Animation

Completed: November 20, 2023

Tools: Procreate, Ipad, Apple Pencil

Service: Animator

goal:

To create an animation of hands sewing.

My mother asked me to create an animation for her nonprofit organisation called Growing A.R.C.. They were going to host an event with another organization and she wanted me to create an animation that would act as a flyer and advertise their event. The animation was posted on their instagram.

challenges:

The biggest challeng with this project was figuring what I wanted to do.

My mother came to me and asked for a sewing animation that included the names of the different organizations. When I first heard her request I didn't think it would be very hard because I had a lot of freedom. As I went to work on it I realized I really wasn't sure what I wanted to do. I eventually settled on making hands that would sew the names of the organizations onto a piece of cloth.

Process:

The process was simple, I would look at reference images and I would look at my own hands.

results:

The hands came out how I wanted them to.

It was a simple clean animation that checked off all of my moms requirements. It was also good practice, very similar to working with a client.
