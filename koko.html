works about contact

details:

Type: Illustration

Completed: May 18, 2020

Tools: Procreate, Ipad, Apple Pencil

Service: Illustrator

goal:

To create an illustration of one of my close friends.

I had just finished illustrating a portrait of myself and I figured why not do an illustration of one of my closest friends as well. We’ve been friends since elementary school and I realized I’ve never tried to draw him so why not?

challenges:

I don’t find copying images very challenging for the most part.

Most of the time the challenging part of art is creating original ideas with meaning.

Process:

Since I was drawing someone and I want the subject to like how they were being depicted, I kept in constant contact with them throughout the illustration.

The first thing I did was sketch the image he gave me as a reference. In hindsight, I really didn't have to do that but I like drawing in my sketchbook, and the sketch took away a lot of unnecessary elements that cluttered the image. From there I colored it in and asked him what he wanted. He wanted to be wearing a varsity jacket so I gave him one. I also added his name in the form of a logo on the chest of the varsity jacket. His name is Kordell Pierre. I also made the K from the Nike hoodie visible to once again connect with his name.

results:

The illustration was clean and we both liked it.

It wasn't anything too complicated but the use of symbolism in the piece was fun. He also liked the logo I made.
