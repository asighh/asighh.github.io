details:

Type: Illustration

Completed: April 21, 2022

Tools: Sketchbook, Lead Pencil, Pinterest

Service: Illustrator

goal:

I like to keep to myself.

I’m comfortable being by myself but being alone all the time gets lonely. So I made a drawing with this concept in mind.

challenges:

I had a concrete grasp on what I wanted to do so there were very few challenges.

Process:

I used reference photos from Pinterest.

I found a couple of reference photos on Pinterest and fused them to get what I wanted. I created a portrait of a man with a wolf mask on. This is supposed to represent the question I was posing myself, am I a lone wolf or a lonely wolf? Is the lone wolf I think I am just a mask I put on to hide from the fact that I’m lonely?

results:

This one came out nicely and it’s because I had a simple but clear idea in my head.

I didn’t go on tangents and endlessly add to my first idea. I stuck with it and it made my life easier. In terms of the question I was posing to myself, am I a lone wolf or a lonely wolf? I think I’m a bit of both, like most things in life nothing is ever black and white.
